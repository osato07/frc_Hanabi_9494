About Us

Mission

We will spread the spark of a
creation culture that will bloom in the future.

Throughout history, craftsmanship has always been a catalyst for change in the world.
Like fireworks, we aspire for our creations not to end as mere flashes of
brilliance but to serve as sparks that ignite continuity into the next generation.
Each spark aims to grow into a bright light, illuminating the path towards the future.
We are committed to fostering such a culture to bloom into the future,
under the name "Hanabi."

Vision / Slogan

Get it, Give it, Hanabit !

Through our activities, we acquire skills.
We then share these skills with the world,
making them shine like fireworks.
By contributing in various ways,
we aim to transform them into better habits.

Values

Empathy

Beyond merely enjoying, we discover profound pleasure through empathy.
This is an essential component for sharing and enhancing each other's successes,
realizing the value of being with truly cherished companions.
Challenging ourselves means transcending the boundaries of "impossibility,"
creating an environment filled with mutual passion.
This journey, grounded in empathy, enriches through the development of deep human connections,
a sense of security, and a sense of belonging within the group.
